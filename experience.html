Experience

I have held numerous small jobs throughout the years while studying, a teaching assistant to an adult education class, a customer service rep in an elctronics store and a sales assistant in a sports shop.

My first full time job was as an apprentice electrician with Mercury Engineering where I worked through the standards based curriculum. As I was considered a competent worker, I was also chosen to learn specialist techniques such as 'Exothermic Welding' as shown in the video below.

Having completed my apprenticeship, I returned to DIT Kevin St and qualified as an Electrical Services Engineer. Before completing my degree, I started working with JV Tierney Consulting Engineers where I completed projects for numerous clients such as the HSE, Trinity College Dublin and Oracle to name but a few.
